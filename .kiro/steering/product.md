# Product Overview

GrowthPilot AI is a B2B SaaS platform for digital marketing consultants managing Meta Ads campaigns. The system automates campaign data collection, provides AI-driven insights, and streamlines client reporting.

## Core Value Proposition

- Centralized client portfolio and campaign management
- Automated Meta Ads data synchronization
- AI-powered daily action plans and optimization strategies
- Commission-based revenue tracking
- One-click client reporting (WhatsApp/PDF formats)
- Creative content generation for multiple industries

## Target Users

Digital marketing consultants and performance marketers who manage multiple client Meta Ads accounts and need to track campaigns, optimize performance, and calculate commission-based revenue.

## Key Features

- Client portfolio management with commission models
- Meta Ads API integration for automatic campaign data import
- Google Gemini AI for actionable recommendations and strategy cards
- Financial dashboard with spending and revenue trends
- Lead quality tracking with conversion feedback
- Industry-specific creative content generator
- Automated daily action plans prioritizing top 3 tasks

## Language & Locale

All UI text, error messages, and generated content are in Turkish. Currency is formatted in Turkish Lira (TRY), dates use DD.MM.YYYY format.
